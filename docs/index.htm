We are a mutual-aid group of NYC cyclists+ providing free delivery service to most at-risk community members during the COVID-19 public health crisis. We have around 350 volunteers located across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens and The Bronx. We will try our best to serve you and your needs.
Do you need a delivery?

We are helping members of our community in NYC who are unable to leave their homes due to COVID-19.

We make no-contact deliveries of groceries, medical supplies, medication, and any other supplies you may need. Please send a request through our contact page.

Notes:

  • We ask that if you can find someone in your household, a friend or a neighbor who can go out and get supplies for you to please contact them prior to using our service.
  • If you can afford to, please consider using existing delivery services such as Instacart, Amazon Fresh or Postmates. During a time with so much financial insecurity, we would like our community to continue to help employed workers as much as possible. Please tip your delivery providers well!

Do you need food assistance?

If your needs are not urgent, we ask for you to please check out nyc.gov/getfood or our resources page for more info. We have provided links to food assistance programs around the city.

​If your needs are urgent, we will try our best to help you out. Our funds are limited and we are just a small group of volunteers, so please be patient. We will happily pickup from a local pantry for you if you need delivery help, just send us the pantry info in your request. To find your local pantry, visit our resources page, as well.

How do we keep everyone safe?

Our couriers are required to do the following to keep themselves and keep recipients as safe as possible:

  • Couriers are required to wear masks
  • Couriers must wear gloves and carry sanitizer on deliveries
  • The entire team monitors their health each day.
  • We remind our couriers to wash/sanitize their hands/equipment regularly.
  • Items must be left on stoops, inside corridors, outside buildings
  • Encourage digital reimbursements

Interested in volunteering?

We are always looking for more volunteers to join our network. Anyone who is capable of making deliveries can join us. We are mainly cyclists, but love to grow our community of volunteers with cars, motorcycles, etc. Please email us to join.
